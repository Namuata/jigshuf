Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Jigshuffle ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Jigshuffle website at jigshuffle.com.

1. Information We Collect

Jigshuffle does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

We store the following data locally in your browser only using the Web Storage API (localStorage). This data never leaves your device:

  • Your game score and current streak
  • Your best streak and total rounds played
  • Your in-session guess history
  • Your selected game category and filter preferences
  • Your Daily Challenge completion status for the current day

This data is stored under keys prefixed with jig_ and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.

2. Cookies and Advertising

Jigshuffle displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing history and interests.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites that use its services
  • Google Ad Settings (opt out of personalised ads)

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising at aboutads.info/choices or youronlinechoices.eu.

3. Third-Party APIs

Jigshuffle loads images and data from the following third-party services. When your browser fetches content from these services, your IP address and standard browser headers are transmitted to them under their own privacy policies.

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — movie and TV show posters. TMDB Privacy Policy
  • PokéAPI — Pokémon artwork. pokeapi.co
  • Jikan / MyAnimeList — anime cover images. MAL Privacy Policy
  • Wikimedia Commons / Wikidata — company logos and character images. Wikimedia Privacy Policy
  • Riot Games Data Dragon — League of Legends champion artwork. Riot Privacy Notice
  • flagcdn.com — country flag images.

4. Analytics

We do not currently use any first-party analytics service. Aggregate usage data may be available through Google AdSense reporting.

5. Children's Privacy

Jigshuffle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use our contact form.
